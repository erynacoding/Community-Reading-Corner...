About Community Reading Corner

The Community Reading Corner is a literacy initiative dedicated to empowering children and youth through reading. We set up reading spaces, organize storytelling events, and collaborate with volunteers to nurture a lifelong love for books.

Our Mission

To improve literacy levels and inspire curiosity by providing access to books and learning resources for all children.

Our Vision

To build a community where every child can read, learn, and dream without limits.

Our Values

Inclusivity, Creativity, Empowerment, and Lifelong Learning.
